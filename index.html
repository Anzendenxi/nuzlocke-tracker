Run rules

Catches per route

  • You may catch one Pokémon from tall grass on each route.
  • You may catch one additional Pokémon from water on each route (separate from the grass encounter).

Legendaries

Legendary Pokémon cannot be caught.

Revives

A fainted Pokémon may be revived only using Sacred Ashes. That item is obtained by defeating gym leaders.

After each gym leader

When a gym leader is defeated, players battle each other using the bracket system below. Points and shop rewards are based on these results.

Bracket players

Four trainers rotate in two opening brackets: Juan, Tirri, Pepo, and Rodri.

Structure that never changes

After the two opening matches, the bracket always continues the same way:

  • Losers’ match: loser of opening bracket 1 vs loser of opening bracket 2.
  • Winners’ match: winner of opening bracket 1 vs winner of opening bracket 2.

Only the opening pairings change from gym to gym; the losers’ and winners’ lines are always labeled like this.

Opening pairings by gym (8 gyms)

This run follows the game’s eight gym leaders. After each leader, you play the bracket once. The two first-round pairings rotate in a 3-step pattern (A → B → C → A → …) — only gyms 1–8 apply here; there is no ninth gym in this ruleset.

Step A — gyms 1, 4, 7

  • Bracket 1: Juan vs Tirri
  • Bracket 2: Pepo vs Rodri

Step B — gyms 2, 5, 8

  • Bracket 1: Juan vs Pepo
  • Bracket 2: Rodri vs Tirri

Step C — gyms 3, 6

  • Bracket 1: Rodri vs Juan
  • Bracket 2: Tirri vs Pepo

Order for the full run: gym 1 (A), 2 (B), 3 (C), 4 (A), 5 (B), 6 (C), 7 (A), 8 (B). The losers’ and winners’ matches after those openings stay the same every time.

Points per gym round (placements)

After both opening brackets and the losers’ / winners’ matches, placements map to points like this:

  • 1st place (winners’ match — winner): 4.5 points
  • 2nd place (winners’ match — loser): 3 points
  • 3rd place (losers’ match — winner): 2 points
  • 4th place (losers’ match — loser): 1.5 points

Safari Zone

Each time a player’s total reaches a multiple of 10 points, they earn one Safari Zone catch.

Bonus catch on the next route

The trainer who wins the winners’ match also gets one extra catch on the next new route the group enters.

Point shop

Spend points earned from the post–gym leader brackets. Prices are fixed; track balances outside this app if you like.
